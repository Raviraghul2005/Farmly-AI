## üé§ Demo Delivery Tips

1. **Speak slowly and clearly**: Judges may not be familiar with agricultural context
2. **Show, don't just tell**: Let the app speak for itself
3. **Handle delays gracefully**: If inference takes >2s, fill time by explaining the model
4. **Make eye contact**: Don't stare at the phone the entire time
5. **Be enthusiastic**: Show genuine passion for solving this problem
6. **Practice Hindi pronunciation**: Don't fumble the voice query
7. **Have a human story**: Reference Ramesh (the farmer persona) throughout
